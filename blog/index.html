After she got married in 2017, she and her husband moved to Edinburgh, United Kingdom. Shina Kim was the music director of Bristo Concert Community Band in Edinburgh for a year and she is currently teaching clarinet, piano and music theory at Morningside Music School in Edinburgh since 2018.
